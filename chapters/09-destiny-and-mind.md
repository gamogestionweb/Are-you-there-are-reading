## The Power of Belief

How we think about destiny affects how we live:

| Belief | Effect on Behavior |
|--------|-------------------|
| "Everything is random" | Less purpose, less motivation |
| "Everything happens for a reason" | More resilience, more meaning |
| "I create my own destiny" | More agency, more responsibility |
| "Fate is fixed" | Less anxiety about outcomes |

**In a way, destiny is a psychological tool.**
