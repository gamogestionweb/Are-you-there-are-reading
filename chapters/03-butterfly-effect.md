# Chapter III
## The Unexpected Accident
### The Butterfly Effect in Real Life

<p align="center">
  <em>"Seemingly insignificant moments that trigger events capable of changing history"</em>
</p>

---

Life is full of seemingly insignificant moments that, when intertwined, trigger events capable of changing the course of history.

---

## Case Studies

### 1. The Assassination Attempt on Donald Trump

<table>
<tr>
<td width="100" align="center">
<h2>2024</h2>
<sub>Butler, PA</sub>
</td>
<td>

**July 13, 2024** — During a rally in Butler, Pennsylvania, former president [Donald Trump](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Trump) was the victim of an assassination attempt.

- An attacker fired **eight times**
- Trump wounded in his right ear
- [Secret Service](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Secret_Service) killed the assailant on site

**September 15, 2024** — Trump survived a **second** assassination attempt in West Palm Beach, Florida.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

These events highlight how a series of decisions and coincidences can converge, putting lives at risk and altering political landscapes.

### 2. The Assassination of Franz Ferdinand

<table>
<tr>
<td width="100" align="center">
<h2>1914</h2>
<sub>Sarajevo</sub>
</td>
<td>

**June 28, 1914** — Archduke [Franz Ferdinand of Austria](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Archduke_Franz_Ferdinand_of_Austria) and his wife [Sophie](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sophie,_Duchess_of_Hohenberg) were visiting Sarajevo.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

```
    TIMELINE OF FATE
    ─────────────────

    10:00   Assassination plot prepared by Serbian nationalists
            ↓
    10:15   FIRST ATTEMPT: Bomb thrown at motorcade
            ↓
            ✗ FAILED — Bomb bounced off car, injured bystanders
            ↓
    10:30   Franz Ferdinand continues with schedule
            ↓
    11:00   Driver takes WRONG TURN
            ↓
    11:01   Car stalls in front of Moritz Schiller's café
            ↓
    11:02   Gavrilo Princip happens to be standing there
            ↓
            ✓ Princip shoots Franz Ferdinand and Sophie
            ↓
            ↓
    ═══════════════════════════════════════════════
            WORLD WAR I BEGINS
            17 million dead
    ═══════════════════════════════════════════════
```

> If the driver hadn't changed route, or if [Gavrilo Princip](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gavrilo_Princip) hadn't been in that place at that moment, the war might not have started the way it did.

*As we mentioned in [Chapter I](01-threshold-of-existence.md), these events seem like "jokes of existence"*

### 3. The Baltimore Bridge Collapse

<table>
<tr>
<td width="100" align="center">
<h2>2024</h2>
<sub>Baltimore</sub>
</td>
<td>

**March 2024** — A cargo ship struck the [Francis Scott Key Bridge](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_Scott_Key_Bridge_(Baltimore)), causing its total collapse.

**Critical detail:** Minutes before the impact, traffic had been stopped due to an electrical problem on the ship.

This prevented a **much larger number of victims**.

</td>
